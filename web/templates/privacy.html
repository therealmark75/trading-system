Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026  |  Data controller: Mark Nicholson Consulting Limited T/A The Signal Vault

1. What Data We Collect

We collect the following personal data when you create an account or use our platform:

  • Account data: email address, username, and hashed password
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, watchlist contents, and filter preferences
  • Contact data: name, email, and message content submitted via our contact form

We do not collect payment card data directly. Payment processing is handled by Stripe, which has its own privacy policy.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To operate and deliver the SignalIntel platform
  • To send email alerts you have opted into
  • To respond to support and contact form submissions
  • To improve the platform based on usage patterns
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on servers based in the UK. We use SQLite for user account data and application state. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectify inaccurate personal data
  • Erase your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict processing of your personal data
  • Port your data to another service
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@thesignalvault.io.

5. Cookies

We use session cookies for authentication only. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies. Browser localStorage is used to store your UI preferences (e.g. collapsed sections) — this data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

6. Contact for Privacy Queries

For privacy-related enquiries, please email hello@thesignalvault.io.

Mark Nicholson Consulting Limited is the data controller for all personal data processed through this platform.
