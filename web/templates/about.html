The Platform

SignalIntel

SignalIntel is the core product of The Signal Vault. It aggregates data from multiple sources — FinViz, SEC EDGAR, Financial Modelling Prep, and proprietary scrapers — to generate composite signal scores for thousands of US-listed stocks. Each stock receives a 7-tier rating from Strong Buy to Strong Sell, updated daily, with full score breakdowns covering momentum, quality, insider activity, mean reversion, and legal risk.
