TECHNICAL INDICATORS
RSI(14) Relative Strength Index. Below 30 = oversold (potential buy). Above 70 = overbought (potential sell). 50 = neutral.
SMA 50 % above/below 50-day moving average. Positive = price above trend (bullish). Negative = price below trend (bearish).
SMA 200 % above/below 200-day moving average. The long-term trend indicator. Positive = long-term bullish.
52W High % below the 52-week high price. Near 0% = at or near peak. -20% = 20% off its high.
52W Low % above the 52-week low price. +50% = trading 50% above its yearly floor, showing strong recovery.
Analyst Rec Consensus analyst recommendation. Scale: 1 = Strong Buy, 2 = Buy, 3 = Hold, 4 = Sell, 5 = Strong Sell.
