Score Components (0–100)
Momentum
Price trend strength based on RSI, moving average crossovers, and recent price performance. High = strong upward trend. A score above 70 suggests significant momentum.
Quality
Fundamental strength — P/E ratio, profit margins, ROE, EPS growth, and debt levels. High = strong business with solid financials. Sector-adjusted where possible.
Insider
Recent insider buying activity. 100 = significant cluster buying by company executives — the strongest conviction signal available. Required for STRONG BUY tier.
Reversion
Mean reversion opportunity. High = stock has deviated far from its historical mean and may snap back. A reversion score ≥ 75 triggers the HOLD rating regardless of composite.
